# Jobsmith

Jobsmith is a local-first job search assistant for iOS. It finds job postings,
scores them against your background, tailors a resume and cover letter, and
helps you fill out applications.

It has no accounts and no servers of its own. Your profile, your resume, and the
jobs you have found live on your device.
